About Us

Our clients (Attached List) are confident in operating on-line Dairy Management system. This system is Windows based and it can be implemented for Multi Company, Multi Year and Multi User (LAN Base).

We have successfully implemented our ERP system to Milk Unions as well as Private Dairies having their turn-over up to few hundred crores. We have a range of Dairy Softwares Starting from small chilling plants to big production making plants having their turn-over from few lacs to few hundred crores. We are glad to say that our software system is successfully running at more than 100 small level plants More than 20 big dairies in Maharashtra Goa with customer satisfaction.

We can accept and fulfill the challenging jobs particularly in dairy segment. On-line milk procurement (i.e. Direct Interface of Weighing Scale and Eco-Milk to PC) will benefit in no-human errors & interfere. That will save lot of time with more accuracy. We have User Creation Facility .In this we have maintained full security at all user-levels. Authority to users can be set by the admin person depending upon designation of the user. The user can have Add, Modify and Delete options. All users must keep their user name and password confidential.

We have a Multi-Company facility if all companies having same activities & nature of business. You can create any number of companies by your own. Easy Year-Ending process that will prepare new year database with current balances c/f within few seconds just by entering password. Easy Carry-Forward process that will calculate balances of last year accounts and will carry forward to current(New) year even after year ending. We have used Auto-Accounts Creation Concept at the time of creating new society account. That will create multiple sub accounts the time of creating a new society account. All accounts auto created like Advance A/c, Cattle feed A/c, etc. have their internal relationships and are inter-related. There are many Sub-Societies that are supplying milk under one society this relationship & concept is handled very carefully in this system till finalisations.
